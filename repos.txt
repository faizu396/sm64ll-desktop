This is a list of repos (and their branches) that work with sm64ll-pi.

sm64ex: the fork that started it all.
	URL: 
	https://github.com/sm64pc/sm64ex
	
	Branches:
	nightly: Main Branch, actively being developed.
	master: Outdated, use Nightly instead.
	
sm64ex-coop: a fork of sm64ex with online multiplayer.
	URL: https://github.com/djoslin0/sm64ex-coop
	
	Branches:
	unstable: The only branch.
	
Render96ex: Render96 is a project dedicated to modding sm64 to look just like the promotional renders / art of the time.
	URL: https://github.com/Render96/Render96ex/tree/master
	
	Branches:
	master: Normal sm64ex, requires the texture and model packs for the proper experience. (Auto texture and modelpack setup coming soon).
	alpha: a repo where the developers upload work-in-progress code.
